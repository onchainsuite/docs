## General

### Who is Onchain Suite for?

It is built for Web3 growth, lifecycle, CRM, and operations teams that want to act on wallet and product behavior without stitching together a patchwork stack.

### Do I need email identity before I can get value?

No. Onchain Suite is wallet-first, which means a connected wallet can still be a useful profile even before an email is linked.

### Do I need SQL to use the platform?

No. SQL is valuable for advanced cohort work, but many teams start with segments, reports, campaigns, and pre-built plays.

## Setup

### What should I configure first?

Start with project settings, sender verification, domain authentication, and the integrations needed for your first audience.

### What is the best first workflow?

A small internal campaign, a narrow audience segment, or one simple automation is usually the fastest way to validate your setup.

## Channels

### Is Onchain Suite email-only?

No. Email is one channel, but the platform is designed for multi-channel activation, including in-app experiences and future channel expansion.
