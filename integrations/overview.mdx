## What Integrations Unlock

Integrations bring wallet data, contract behavior, product events, and delivery channels into one operating model so campaigns and automations can react in real time.

## Core Integration Areas

- Wallet and contract data sources
- Product or backend webhooks that enrich lifecycle logic
- Publishable and secret keys for secure client and server use
- In-app delivery surfaces and SDK-based experiences
- Third-party systems that support CRM, analytics, or growth operations

## Recommended Adoption Pattern

Start with the data and channel needed for one high-value workflow. Once that loop works, expand to additional triggers, channels, and team systems.
