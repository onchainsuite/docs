## Why In-App Matters

In-app delivery is often the highest-leverage channel in Onchain Suite because it reaches connected wallets without DNS setup, inbox warm-up, or prior email matching.

## How It Fits The Platform

A typical in-app flow looks like this:

1. Your app requests a challenge for a connected wallet.
2. The wallet signs and verifies.
3. Onchain Suite issues a short-lived in-app session.
4. The client registers and listens for live notification events.
5. Automation or campaign logic pushes the message into the user experience.

## Good Use Cases

- Remind users to finish onboarding.
- Announce claim windows or mint deadlines.
- Reinforce campaign CTAs after an email send.
- Trigger product messages from contract or wallet activity.

## Best Practices

- Treat in-app as part of a broader lifecycle journey, not an isolated alert channel.
- Align in-app timing with campaign and automation ownership.
- Use it early in onboarding when email identity may not exist yet.
