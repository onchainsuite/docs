## Fastest First Win

The best first campaign is usually internal or limited-audience, with a simple objective and one clear success metric.

## Suggested Flow

1. Create a small audience segment.
2. Build a simple email with one primary CTA.
3. Verify sender and domain settings.
4. Send internal previews.
5. Launch to a limited audience or test cohort.
6. Review deliverability and engagement before scaling.
