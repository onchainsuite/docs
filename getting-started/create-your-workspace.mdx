## Overview

Your workspace is the home for your team, projects, permissions, and operational defaults.

## Recommended Setup

1. Create the workspace with your company or protocol name.
2. Add the first project for the brand or environment you want to manage.
3. Invite the teammates who need access to campaigns, settings, or reporting.
4. Decide who owns sender setup, domain setup, and integrations.

## Good Practices

- Keep project names clear and environment-specific.
- Limit admin access to the people handling setup and security.
- Separate test and production work when needed.
