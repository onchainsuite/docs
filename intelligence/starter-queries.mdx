---
title: "Starter Queries"
description: "Use starter queries to get to a useful audience or report faster."
---

## Useful Starting Points

- Recently active wallets
- Dormant holders
- Recent minters with no follow-up action
- High-value users with low recent engagement
- Users who clicked but did not convert

## Workflow

1. Pick the closest starter query.
2. Adjust the filters.
3. Validate the results.
4. Save the query as a report or segment.
