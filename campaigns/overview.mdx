## What Campaigns Are For

Campaigns are best for one-time or scheduled outbound messages such as launches, governance reminders, rewards notices, or lifecycle nudges.

## Typical Lifecycle

1. Define the audience.
2. Build or select the email.
3. Preview and test.
4. Schedule or launch.
5. Monitor results and follow-up.
