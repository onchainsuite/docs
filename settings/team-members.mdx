## Recommended Roles

- Admins manage project setup and security.
- Operators manage campaigns and automations.
- Analysts review intelligence and reporting.
- Contributors draft content and support reviews.

## Good Practices

- Grant the least access needed for each role.
- Review access after major team changes.
- Keep ownership clear for senders, domains, and integrations.
