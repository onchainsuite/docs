## Records You May Need

Most sending setups rely on some combination of SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records.

## Tips

- Copy each record exactly as shown.
- Make changes in the correct DNS zone.
- Allow propagation time before rechecking.
- Consolidate conflicting legacy records when needed.
