## What These Signals Help You Do

Health and churn views help teams move from raw activity to prioritization.

## Typical Questions

- Which users are cooling off and need follow-up?
- Which high-value wallets should enter a win-back play?
- Which audience should receive a campaign versus an automation branch?

## Recommendation

Use health and churn signals as prioritization inputs alongside contract behavior, campaign response, and product context rather than as isolated scores.
