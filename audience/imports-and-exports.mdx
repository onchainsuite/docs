## Imports

Imports are useful when you need to seed audience data from existing CRM, user, or partner datasets.

## Exports

Exports help teams share operational outputs, build reports, or hand data off to other systems.

## Good Practices

- Decide on your source of truth before large imports.
- Keep field mapping and deduplication rules consistent.
- Export only the data a workflow truly needs.
