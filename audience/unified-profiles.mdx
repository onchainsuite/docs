## Profile Model

A profile can include wallet addresses, channel handles, tags, attributes, last activity, and engagement or health signals.

## Why This Matters

Unified profiles make it easier to answer questions like:

- Can this wallet be reached in-app, by email, or both?
- What was the last meaningful product or onchain action?
- Which users look healthy, cooling, or at risk?

## Recommendation

Treat profiles as operating records for lifecycle work, not just as static contact rows.
