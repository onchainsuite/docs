## What Audience Means In Onchain Suite

Audience is the operational layer that turns raw user and wallet data into people you can understand, segment, and message.

## What Lives Here

- Unified profiles that combine identity, channel, and behavioral context
- Saved segments for campaigns and automations
- Import and export workflows
- Health and churn signals that help prioritize follow-up

## Why It Matters

In Web3, a wallet is not enough and an email list is not enough. Audience gives your team a usable operating model across both.
