---
title: "Connect to Campaigns"
description: "Hand users from automation logic into campaign-style messaging or follow-up flows."
---

## Good Handoff Patterns

- Trigger an email after a segment entry.
- Queue a follow-up campaign after an in-app message.
- Create a win-back branch for inactive users.
- Send a governance reminder to a contract-derived cohort.

## Recommendation

Document the intended path before building so lifecycle and campaign teams stay aligned on timing and audience ownership.
